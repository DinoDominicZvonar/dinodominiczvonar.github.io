Heronin

“Four player chaos. Two player duels. One-hit-kill. Shoot, dash, and slash to annihilate your friends(?) in this intense platform fighterFour player chaos. Two player duels. One-hit-kill. Shoot, dash, and slash to annihilate your friends(?) in this intense platform fighter”

Heronin was the last project I worked on while studying at The Game Assembly. Heronin was developed over the span of 4 weeks in our own custom game engine called Aurora. The Game took inspiration from Samurai Gunn 2



My Contributions

  • Implementation of xbox controller support, supporting multiple controllers at the same time

  • Player management, making it possible for up to 4 players to play with either a controller or keyboard

  • Implementation of a base Player Movement component together with Ozzy Joelsson

  • Sword and Bow combat

  • Implementation of User Controlled players

  • Engine, Editor and Gameplay related bugfixes

  • Sound and Music implementation
