Fay

“You play as a internet vlogger named Fay, you've gotten an e-mail from a fan about a haunted house. You chose to explore it. Can you help Fay escape? Or will she be stuck there forever?"

This project was made as my graduation project from my upper secondary school LBS (Ljud Och Bildskolan). This game was developed off and on for about six months with me doing everything from the graphics and the sounds to the coding.
