Atlantean Ascent

“You're a Fisherman trapped in an underwater metropolis. Harpoon through the fish monsters and get to the surface.”

Our seventh project at The Game Assembly is a third person shooter. Atlantean Ascent was developed over the span of 14 weeks in our custom engine called Aurora.

Atlantean Ascent was inspired by Remnant 2 and Necropolis.
